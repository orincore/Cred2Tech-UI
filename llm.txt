# BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
# ============================================================================

Sunby Credtech Private Limited is a Bengaluru-based fintech company that 
operates Cred2Tech, India's leading MSME credit platform. Sunby Credtech 
provides instant business loan eligibility checks, DSA (Direct Selling Agent) 
partner network solutions, and multi-lender matching for businesses seeking 
loans from HDFC Bank, Axis Bank, Kotak Mahindra, Yes Bank, IDFC First Bank, 
and other leading financial institutions.

Sunby Credtech's AI-powered technology simplifies MSME lending by:
- Matching businesses with ideal lenders in minutes
- Providing comprehensive eligibility checks across 50+ lenders
- Offering a complete CRM platform for DSA agents
- Enabling GST, ITR, and bank statement-based loan assessments

Founded by Bobby Thomas M and Sunil Agarwal, Sunby Credtech brings 40+ years 
of combined financial services experience and has managed ₹2000+ Crore in 
loan portfolios.
